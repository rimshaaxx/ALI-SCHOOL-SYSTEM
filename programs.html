Academic Programs

At Ali Kids Campus, our academic programs are designed to spark curiosity, build foundational skills, and prepare students for a dynamic future. We believe in joyful learning that balances structure with creativity, and rigor with empathy.

Programs by Age Group

Preschool (3–5)

Play-based learning, motor skills, early literacy, and social development in a nurturing environment.

Grades 1–5

Core subjects, creative arts, and character-building activities.

Grades 6–8

Advanced literacy, project-based science, leadership development, and digital skills.

Grades 9–10

Career prep, electives, civic engagement, and real-world problem solving.

Our Learning Approach

🎯. Inquiry-Based Learning

Students ask questions, explore ideas, and build knowledge through discovery.

🎯. Hands-On Activities

Learning through experiments, art, building, and real-world simulations.

🎯. Collaborative Projects

Group work that builds teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

🎯. Personalized Support

Teachers adapt lessons to meet each student’s strengths and needs.

🎯. Real-World Connections

Lessons tied to current events, community issues, and global challenges.

A Day in the Life of Our Students

7:00 AM – Morning Arrival

Students arrive, greet teachers, and settle in for the day.

7:30 AM – Morning Assembly

We begin with a thought of the day, announcements, and a cheerful start.

8:00 AM – Core Subjects

Focused learning in English, Math, and Science using Oxford curriculum.

10:30 AM – Break & Social Time

Students relax, play, and connect with friends in a safe outdoor space.

11:00 AM – Creative Periods

Art, music, or computer lab — where imagination comes alive.

12:30 PM – Wrap-Up & Reflection

Students journal, share highlights, and prepare for the next day.

1:00 PM – Dismissal

Students head home after a day of learning and growth.
